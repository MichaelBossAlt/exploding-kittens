📖 How to Play

Objective

Be the last player alive. Avoid drawing an Exploding Kitten — unless you have a Defuse card.

On Your Turn

  • Play any cards you want (optional)
  • Draw 1 card from the deck to end your turn
  • If it's an Exploding Kitten — you're out unless you Defuse it!

Card Types

  • 💥 Exploding Kitten — Dead unless Defused
  • 🛡 Defuse — Cancel an Exploding Kitten
  • 🚫 Nope — Cancel any action (not Defuse). Play anytime!
  • ⏭ Skip — End your turn without drawing
  • ⚔️ Attack — Skip your draw, next player draws 2 (stackable!)
  • 🔀 Shuffle — Shuffle the draw deck
  • 👁 See the Future — Peek at top 3 cards (private)
  • 🎁 Favor — Force a player to give you 1 card
  • 🐱 Cat Cards — Play 2 matching = steal a random card from any player

Nope Rules

Nope cancels any action card. Another player can Nope the Nope (making it a Yep). You can chain Nopes!

Attack Stacking

If the forced player also plays Attack, the next player must draw the accumulated total (2+2=4, etc.).
